About Our Hospital

Jawahar Eye & Ent Hospital & Laser Center (A Unit Of Jawahar Eye Hospital Private Limited) was established in the year 1996. It is a specialized eye hospital that offers a wide range of eye services. The hospital is known for its team of qualified and experienced doctors and staff who are dedicated to providing the highest quality of care.

At Jawahar Eye & Ent Hospital & Laser Center, patients can expect to receive the latest and most advanced techniques in the field of ophthalmology. The hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and equipment, ensuring accurate diagnoses and effective treatments.

With a focus on delivering comprehensive eye care, the hospital offers services such as routine eye examinations, cataract surgery, LASIK surgery, glaucoma treatment, corneal transplantation, and many more. The doctors at Jawahar Eye & Ent Hospital & Laser Center specialize in various sub-specialties of ophthalmology, ensuring that patients receive the most appropriate and personalized treatment for their specific condition. The hospital’s dedication to patient care extends beyond providing high-quality medical services. The staff at Jawahar Eye & Ent Hospital & Laser Center are known for their compassionate and caring approach, making the hospital a comfortable and reassuring place for patients.

Overall, Jawahar Eye & Ent Hospital & Laser Center is a trusted and reliable choice for anyone in need of specialized eye care. With its commitment to excellence and patient satisfaction, the hospital continues to be a leader in the field of ophthalmology.
